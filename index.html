About VLoop India

VLoop India is committed to building a cleaner and greener future through innovative recycling solutions. We specialize in PET Plastic and EPS (Thermocol) recycling, helping industries and communities reduce waste, recover valuable materials, and support a sustainable circular economy.

Our Recycling Services

PET Recycling

Collection, segregation, processing and recycling of PET plastic waste into valuable reusable resources.

EPS / Thermocol Recycling

Responsible recycling solutions for Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) and Thermocol waste materials.

Our Mission

To transform waste into value, conserve natural resources and create a sustainable ecosystem for future generations.
