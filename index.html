About Us

VLoop India is dedicated to creating a cleaner and more sustainable future through responsible recycling solutions. We specialize in PET Plastic and EPS (Thermocol) recycling, helping industries and communities reduce waste and support a circular economy.

Our Recycling Services

PET Recycling

Collection, sorting, processing and recycling of PET plastic waste into reusable resources for sustainable manufacturing.

EPS / Thermocol Recycling

Environmentally responsible recycling solutions for Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) and Thermocol waste materials.

Our Mission

Transform waste into value while protecting natural resources and building a cleaner future for generations to come.
